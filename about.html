Company Profile

Company Profile

Incorporated in 1994, Adorn Engineering is an ISO 9001:2008 certified engineering company based at Ahmedabad, India. Adorn Engineering is manufacturer and exporter of quality and precision products in the category of Weighing Equipment, Material Handling Equipment, Bag Filling Equipment and Pre Engineering Building systems.

The company is promoted by the professionals with around 20 years of experience in the Industry. Adorn designs and develops custom built and tailor made equipments to suit the customer’s specific requirement. All the equipments are manufactured using quality raw material and bought out items procured from reputed suppliers. Experienced and dedicated team of professionals is employed to manufacture the equipments that deliver productivity, return on investment & cost effectiveness to its prestigious clientele. Our equipments are Durable, Economical, Operator Friendly, Efficient, Technologically Updated, Easy to Install and Maintain. Ready availability of spare parts & prompt after sales service are the key to our business development.

Mission Statement

Adorn is committed to provide range of quality products and services in the field of Weighing, Material Handling and Industrial Shed Construction.

To provide good quality products and services in compliance to customer’s requirement that is fairly priced, delivered on time and backed up with prompt after sales service.

Vision Statement

To be Recognized Engineering Company in Domestic and International Market for Providing Comprehensive Solutions and Services in the field of Weighing, Material Handling, Industrial Shed Construction and Bag Filling.
